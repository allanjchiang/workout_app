Overview

Workout Tracker ("the App") is a simple fitness application that helps you track your resistance band exercises. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App stores your workout data (exercise names and rep counts) locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device.

Data Storage

  • All workout logs and exercise data are stored locally on your device using your device's built-in storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No data is uploaded to the cloud
  • No data is shared with third parties

Data Sharing & Selling

We do not share or sell your data. Since all data remains on your device, there is nothing to share or sell.

Permissions

The App does not require any special permissions to function. It only uses:

  • Local device storage to save your workout history
  • Audio playback for the rest timer notification

Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Export: You can export your workout data at any time using the share button in the App
  • Delete: You can delete all your data at any time using the delete button in the App
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the App will remove all stored data from your device

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App is designed for general fitness tracking and does not target children.
