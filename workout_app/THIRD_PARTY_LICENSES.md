## Viewing Licenses In-App

Users can view all third-party licenses directly in the app by:
1. Tapping the ℹ️ (info) button in the app bar
2. Tapping "View Open Source Licenses" in the About dialog

This uses Flutter's built-in `showLicensePage()` which automatically displays license information for all packages.
